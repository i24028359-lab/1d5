About Chifeng

Chifeng City is located in the southeast of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. It is the largest city by population in Inner Mongolia and also the economic center of eastern Inner Mongolia. Chifeng has a long history and profound cultural heritage, with rich natural resources and cultural landscapes. Here you can find vast grasslands, spectacular deserts, mysterious Hongshan culture, and hospitable Mongolian people.

As digital tourism ambassadors, we are committed to showcasing the unique charm of Chifeng to the world through digital media, letting more people know about this beautiful city.
